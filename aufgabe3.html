My CATypes

tabby white

A tabby is any dpmestic cat(Felis catus) that has a coat featuring distinctive stripes, dots, lines or swirling patterns, always together with a mark resembling an 'M' on its forehead.

russian blue

The Russian Blue is a cat breed that comes in colors varying from a light shimmering silver to a darker slate grey.

white

White cat breeds are fascinating and exotic and some can play fetch, bury their toys or swim.
